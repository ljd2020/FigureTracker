Welcome to Figure Tracker

Your personal anime figure collection manager — track what you own, what's on order, and plan your next finds.
Here's a quick tour to get you started.

🗃️
Owned Tab
Log every figure in your collection. Record the title, character, brand, type, scale, release date, landed cost (price + shipping), purchase site, and the month you paid. Switch between Table and Gallery views. Filter by type, brand, or site, and click any column header to sort.
📦
Pre-orders Tab
Track figures you've ordered but haven't received yet. Store the best price found, estimated shipping, and expected release date. When a figure arrives, click ✓ Recv to move it straight to your Owned collection — it auto-fills all the details.
🎯
Character Hunts
Click the + button next to the tab bar and type a character name to create a Hunt. A dedicated tab appears showing every figure of that character across your Owned, Pre-orders, and a personal Wishlist. Use the progress bar to see how complete your collection is. Add wished items and promote them to Pre-order or Owned whenever you're ready to buy.
📊
Analytics Tab
Get a live dashboard of your spending. See total figures owned, total spend, average landed cost, and pending pre-order value at a glance. Charts show monthly spend & cumulative totals, breakdown by figure type, purchase site, top brands by spend, and a price distribution histogram.
💾
Export & Import
All data is saved in your browser's local storage — nothing is sent to a server. Use Export (in the Owned tab toolbar) to download a full backup as a .json file including all images. Use Import to restore from that file on any device or browser. Export regularly to keep a backup of your collection.
🖼️
Adding Images
When adding or editing a figure, paste an image URL or upload a file from your device. Uploaded images are automatically resized and stored locally. Click any thumbnail to open a full-size lightbox view.
